YOGA

This class fuses traditional vinyasa yoga methodology with contemporary dynamic flow. Yoga asanas raise the body's heat and a strong emphasis on linking breath to movement creates a steady and consistent flow from pose to pose.

This powerful fluid exercise format works inner and outer alignment, strength and flexibility moving energy to produce a state of inner calm and balance. All this set to great music, including rock, reggae, hip hop, blues and world beats.

flow base

Our yoga flow base offers you a chance to explore a creative slower paced flow format versus our open yoga flow. Focusing more on flexibility and relaxation, you will find yourself spending more time in postures and transitions while using your breath as your guide. This class is accessible to beginner students but, also fantastic for intermediate or advanced levels looking to deepen their practice.

flow open
