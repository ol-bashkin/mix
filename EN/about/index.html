ABOUT

US

At MIX we believe that working out is about more than just breaking a sweat. We are a dynamic boutique style fitness space offering a powerful and inspirational results producing fitness experience.

Each discipline offers a full body workout and are all geared to work together bringing fun filled energy, professional level of instruction, high intensity all driven by great music!!

Our

Teachers

We believe our trainers should be the best out there so, we train our teachers one by one through an extensive program and ongoing continuing education. When you enter a class, you’re trusting that you will be challenged and engaged, but you’re also trusting that your body will be kept safe. All our classes are run by specialists in their field in order to ensure our clients get their best workout.

Our

Classes

We have developed a mixture of class structures which integrate a results-producing system to tone and reshape the body and center the mind. Our classes will run from early morning to the evening designed to fit in with busy schedules and will be suitable for all fitness levels.

We set out to make it as easy as possible and all classes can be purchased and booked directly online. No contracts! No hassle!

Our

Space

Our unique space and classes are a place to free your mind, connect with your body, find your passion and be a part of our inspiring community.

We focused our attention to quality and detail in every aspect both in and out of the class. Providing a boutique style design with high-end quality sound, showers, amenities and a retail section with MIX branded and other brands of apparel and accessories.
